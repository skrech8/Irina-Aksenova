Artist bio

Irina Aksenova (b. 1958, Kazan) is a contemporary artist known for delicate watercolor and pastel-toned works—portraits and nature studies that balance softness with precision.

Her works have been exhibited in Germany, Austria, Russia, and internationally. The oeuvre includes more than 200 artworks across watercolor, mixed media, and works on paper.

Original artworks are available for collectors worldwide. Commissions are considered for residential and hospitality spaces, with a Dubai representative available for the Middle East.
